Why do we want to make lemons from lemonade? It is all about Alex. There was this awesome kid who passed away from cancer. She had a dream ... a vision to eradicate childhood cancer! She never got to see that dream come true, but others have picked up where she left off.

Alex started a lemonade stand to be able to raise money to give to other kids. She wanted to pass on the gift she had been given from all the people who had helped her. Her mission fighting childhood cancer, one cup at a time.
